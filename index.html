About The Training Institute

Simba Eduzone is an educational and training institute focused towards student’s career in supporting their dreams come true to become Doctors and Engineers. Very educated faculties at Simba Eduzone are passionate about teaching, have got the complete knowledge of NEET and IIT JEE syllabus. Training and coaching for the NEET and IIT JEE entrance exams are tailored to address both needs of CBSE, State Board and ICSE students. Our institute is driven by Professors, Management Professionals and Team with 9 plus years of experience in Healthcare industry. This helps the institution to provide high quality education and well-designed coaching programs to meet all the needs of medical and engineering aspirants.

Why Choose Us
Simba Eduzone

Highly educated and experienced faculties

Simba Eduzone faculties are Phd and M.Sc holders from reputed institutions. We have more than 10 years of teaching experience and 9+ years of experience in the healthcare domain. Every faculty has the dedication towards teaching and pure willingness to build the career of the kids as their own kids.

Limited Students Per Batch

The training and coaching are well planned and ensured that we have limited students per session in order to give maximum concentration to each student in the batch. It gives faculties a clear view on which of the students needs attention and provide the best support to clear all their queries.

Fixed schedule

Well designed schedules for each subjects help students to plan the academics as well as prepare well for the upcoming coaching classes. Parents also gets the clear information on topics in each of subjects which would be covered in each of the schedules.

Simple teaching with practical examples and Video sessions

Teaching tailored to meet the needs of the students. Topics are covered with practical examples and nano videos which helps students to relate the concepts to real life situations, helping to get deeper understanding of the subjects.

Periodic Assessments

Topic based tests, Lesson based tests, monthly assessments, quarterly assessment and multiple mock test prepares students for the final competitive exam

Competitive Price

Cost of studying has become challenging for many students to take up the dream of becoming Doctors and Engineers. Course fee at Simba Eduzone would make every student's career aspiration most affordable.
